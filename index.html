Clicker

Control your Mac's Option key from your iPhone

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 10.15 or later

How It Works

1

Run Clicker on your Mac

Download and unzip. Right-click the app and select Open (required for first launch). Grant Accessibility permission when prompted.

2

Scan the QR code

Point your iPhone camera at the QR code shown on your Mac. Tap the notification to open.

3

Tap to toggle

Tap anywhere on the screen to hold down the Option key. Tap again to release.

Scan the QR code with your iPhone camera

Perfect for:

Extended Option-key tasks, accessing hidden menu options, copying files instead of moving them, or any time you need to hold Option without touching your keyboard.

Requirements: Mac running macOS 10.15+, iPhone with iOS 15+, both on the same WiFi network.

To quit: Open Activity Monitor, search for "Clicker", select it, and click the X button.
